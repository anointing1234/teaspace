About Us

We are an aerospace company with a reputation for quality, technical excellence, and unmatched customer support. We offer premium quality products and services in aerospace engineering, manufacturing, turbine engine and component MRO, and fire protection systems across our Commercial and Defence capabilities. We help customers from airlines, regional commuters, freighters, and general aviation, to agricultural operators and defence forces around the world.

Rely on us for quality and safety

Quality, safety and service are our top priorities at TAE Aerospace. To support the highest quality and safety standards, we maintain a broad suite of regulatory approvals and partnerships with multiple OEMs.

See our full list of regulatory and OEM approvals

Rely on us for technical expertise

Customers and OEMs recognize that our aerospace products and services are delivered by a highly-skilled technical workforce. We are the world’s largest Honeywell-authorized TPE331 engine support provider, and the only Licensed Repair Service Facility for TPE331 fuel controls with both Woodward and Honeywell approvals.

Rely on us for 24/7 support

Our commitment is to outstanding quality work delivered with responsive customer service, 24/7. We provide timely service and support to our customers across North America, Latin America, Canada, Europe, Africa, Australia, New Zealand, and South East Asia.

We are proud that companies like General Electric, Pratt & Whitney, Honeywell, L3Harris, Collins Aerospace and Boeing; and defence forces such as the United States Air Force, Royal Australian Air Force, Australian Army, Royal Malaysian Air Force, United States Navy, Royal Thai Navy, and United States National Guard, all place their trust in us.
