Working at TAE Aerospace

We are recognised around the world as an aerospace company that creates value through our innovative thinking and exceptional quality products and services. At TAE Aerospace, we recognise that business success is built on the depth of knowledge, capability and commitment of employees, and a culture that fosters creative thinking, collaboration and engagement.

We proudly support ADF Reserves and Veterans through our Gold Membership with the Australia Defence Force Reserves and Employer Support Program, including paid Defence Reservist Leave.

With our large scope of programs including engine maintenance, airline wheels and brakes, fuel controls and components, electromechanical components, fire protection and rescue systems, test cell design and upgrades, advanced manufacturing and specialised engineering, we offer a variety of career opportunities in servicing our defence and commercial customers.

Our commitment—rely on us to keep you flying—is at the core of who we are and how we go about our work. Whether it is safety, quality or support, our people consistently deliver.

Looking after our mates is important to everyone at TAE Aerospace. Through our You First Program, we foster a rewarding, enjoyable and fun work environment that supports safe, healthy and active lifestyles.

We are dedicated to maintaining a highly skilled and talented workforce. Our learning and development program helps employees grow the skills and behaviours needed to perform at a high standard.

We also celebrate outstanding contributions through recognition programs, awards, and employee benefits.

We are committed to an inclusive culture that promotes the skills and capabilities of all people. Everyone has the opportunity to succeed, and diversity enhances our effectiveness and collaboration.

By working together across the globe, investing and providing opportunity for our people, we truly set ourselves apart from the rest.

Be part of something bigger. TAE Aerospace is proud to be part of the ASDAM Group, a leading sovereign defence and aerospace enterprise. This connection expands your career potential and gives you the opportunity to contribute to a broader mission supporting Australia’s defence capability.

Wherever you are in the world, you can rely on us to keep you flying in your career!
