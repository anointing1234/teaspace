About TAE Aerospace

Delivering Excellence in Aerospace Engineering & Support

TAE Aerospace is a global company proudly serving Commercial and Defense customers with world-class MRO capabilities, component repair, and turbine engine services.

With a highly skilled technical workforce and trusted OEM partnerships, we deliver reliable, innovative, and cost-effective solutions that keep your operations flying safely and efficiently.

  • Global Expertise

    Supporting aviation customers in over 11 locations worldwide with unmatched technical capability.

  • Proven Reliability

    Backed by decades of aerospace experience and rigorous quality assurance processes.

Our Capabilities

Comprehensive Aerospace
Support and Maintenance

Fixed Wing

Engine & Component Services

Partner with the world’s largest TPE331 engine service provider for maintenance, repair, and training. Expertise includes PT6A, H80 engines, wheels, brakes, and fire protection systems.

Rotary Wing

Helicopter Power Systems
